## What the TOP CREATORS Do (Common Patterns)

### 1. POST CONSTANTLY
- Multiple posts per day (top pages post 5-10x daily)
- Never miss a day — consistency builds habit
- Users check daily expecting new deals

### 2. REAL DEALS (No Crap)
- Only post genuine savings
- "If it's not dirt cheap, I don't promote it"
- Build trust = repeat followers

### 3. ENGAGE EVERYONE
- Reply to EVERY comment
- Thank people who share
- Create community feeling

### 4. USE VIDEO (Reels/FB Watch)
- Video gets 3-5x more reach than images
- Do unboxings, "in-store" tours, price comparisons
- Live videos at supermarkets perform HUGE

### 5. CROSS-POST TO GROUPS
- Post to 10+ relevant Facebook groups daily
- Community groups = free exponential reach

### 6. BUILD PERSONAL BRAND
- The page admin becomes a "celebrity" in the niche
- Samantha Tolley does TV appearances
- Build trust = people follow YOU, not just the page

### 7. COLLABS WITH BRANDS
- Get paid by brands to feature their deals
- Sponsored posts = $50-500/post once you have following
- Affiliate links on every deal

### 8. GIVEAWAYS AT MILESTONES
- 1K, 5K, 10K followers = giveaway posts
- Drive engagement AND new followers (people tag friends)

---

## THE SECRET SAUCE (From Top Creators)

1. **"Hook" in every post** — Stop the scroll immediately
2. **Specific savings** — "$50 off" not "save money"
3. **Urgency** — "Today only!" "Half price until 6pm"
4. **Local** — Mention local areas (Hervey Bay = gold for us!)
5. **Tag friends CTA** — "Tag someone who needs to know this!"
6. **Questions** — Ask engagement questions in EVERY post
